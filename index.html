A Premium Dining Experience

Welcome to Lazzat Shinwari, Bhalwal's finest destination for authentic, mouth-watering BBQ and traditional Shinwari cuisine. Known for our signature red building and unforgettable flavors, we bring the true essence of traditional cooking right to your table.


Whether you're craving our famous Chicken Shinwari Karahi cooked in pure Desi Ghee, our sizzling Turkish Beef Kebabs, or our crispy Zinger burgers, every dish is prepared with the highest quality ingredients and authentic spices. Join us anytime—we are proudly open 24 hours a day!
